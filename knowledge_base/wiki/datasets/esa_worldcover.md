## Overview
A global land cover product at 10 m resolution classifying the Earth's surface into 11 discrete categories (e.g. tree cover, cropland, built-up, water, mangroves, snow/ice), produced by the European Space Agency.

## Statistics
- Coverage: global
- Resolution: 10 m
- Classes: 11
- Year: 2020

## Known Issues
Reference-label uncertainty is non-trivial: Benavides-Martinez et al. use a 98% baseline-recovery threshold specifically because the remaining 2% gap falls within WorldCover's own inherent labeling uncertainty — i.e. further accuracy gains wouldn't meaningfully exceed the reference product's own mapping accuracy.

## Access
Zanaga et al. 2021, "ESA WorldCover 10 m 2020 v100" — publicly available.
